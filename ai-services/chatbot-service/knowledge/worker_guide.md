# Worker Guide — Getting Started on SBOUP

## Step 1 — Create your account

1. Open the SBOUP app and tap **Create Account**.
2. Enter your full name, email address, and phone number.
3. Select your role: **Skilled Worker**.
4. Add your city or district (location).
5. Tap **Next** and verify your email address from the link sent to your inbox.

Your account is now active. The next step is building your profile.

## Step 2 — Build your profile

Your profile is the foundation of everything on SBOUP. A complete profile
gets higher match scores and appears more credible to employers.

Go to the **Profile** tab and tap **Edit about me**.

### What to fill in

| Section | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| Professional title | Tells employers and the AI what kind of worker you are |
| Bio | A short summary of your experience and what you offer |
| Location | Used to match you with nearby jobs |
| Skills | The most important section — drives your match score |
| Experience | Shows your work history and total months of experience |
| Education | Adds credibility for formal roles |
| Portfolio | Proof of your work — photos, links, documents |
| Expected rate | Used to check salary fit with opportunities |
| Availability | Full-time, part-time, contract, or gig |

### Profile completeness score

Your profile completeness is shown as a percentage. It is calculated as:

- Has professional title → 15%
- Has bio → 10%
- Has location → 10%
- Has at least one skill → 25%
- Has at least one experience entry → 25%
- Has at least one portfolio item → 15%

A profile at 100% completeness gets the best match scores.

## Step 3 — Add your skills

Skills are the most important part of your profile. Each skill has a
proficiency level:

- **Beginner** — you know the basics
- **Intermediate** — you can work independently
- **Advanced** — you are highly capable
- **Expert** — you can teach others

Add every skill you genuinely have. The AI matches your skills against job
requirements — more skills means more matches.

## Step 4 — Discover opportunities

Go to the **Discover Jobs** tab. You will see a feed of opportunities, each
showing your personal match percentage.

- Use the **search bar** to find specific roles or skills.
- Use **filters** to narrow by location, category, experience level, or
  compensation range.
- Tap any opportunity to see the full details and your match breakdown.

## Step 5 — Apply

When you find a good match:

1. Open the opportunity details.
2. Review your match score and breakdown.
3. Tap **Quick Apply** to submit instantly using your profile.
4. Optionally attach a tailored CV or cover letter.
5. Track your application status from your **Dashboard**.

### Application statuses

| Status | What it means |
|---|---|
| Submitted | Your application was received |
| Under Review | The employer is reviewing applications |
| Shortlisted | You have been selected for the next stage |
| Offer Extended | The employer has made you an offer |
| Rejected | You were not selected this time |

## Step 6 — Improve over time

- Check the **Upskill** section to see which skills you are missing for jobs
  you want.
- Complete learning paths to add new skills to your profile.
- Keep your profile updated as you gain new experience.
- The more you apply, the better the AI understands your preferences.
