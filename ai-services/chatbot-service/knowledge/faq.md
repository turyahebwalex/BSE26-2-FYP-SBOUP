# Frequently Asked Questions — SBOUP

## Account & Registration

**Q: How do I create an account?**
Tap "Create Account" on the home screen. Enter your name, email, and phone
number, select your role (Skilled Worker or Employer), add your location,
and verify your email. The whole process takes under 2 minutes.

**Q: I did not receive my verification email. What do I do?**
Check your spam or junk folder first. If it is not there, go to the login
screen and tap "Resend verification email". If you still do not receive it,
contact support through the Help section.

**Q: Can I have both a worker and employer account?**
No — each account has one role. If you need both, create two separate
accounts with different email addresses.

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password", enter your email address, and
follow the link sent to your inbox.

**Q: Is SBOUP free to use?**
Yes — creating an account, building a profile, browsing jobs, and applying
is free for workers. Employers pay a subscription to post opportunities.

## Profile & Skills

**Q: Why is my profile completeness low?**
Profile completeness is based on five sections: title, bio, location, skills,
experience, and portfolio. Go to your Profile tab and fill in any missing
sections. Each section has a specific weight — skills (25%) and experience
(25%) have the biggest impact.

**Q: How many skills should I add?**
Add every skill you genuinely have. There is no maximum. More skills means
more potential matches. The AI only counts skills that overlap with a job's
requirements, so extra skills do not hurt you.

**Q: Can I add skills I am still learning?**
Only add skills you can currently use in a job. If you are still learning,
complete the learning path first, then add the skill at Beginner level.

**Q: How do I delete a skill?**
Go to Profile → Edit Skills, tap the skill you want to remove, and tap Delete.

## Match Scores

**Q: Why is my match score low for a job I think I am good for?**
The most common reasons are: (1) your skill names do not exactly match the
job's required skills — check the spelling, (2) you are missing some required
skills, (3) your location does not match and the job is not remote, or
(4) your expected rate is outside the job's compensation range.

**Q: Why do several different jobs all show the same score?**
This usually means you have zero skill overlap with all of them. The small
baseline score comes from profile completeness. Add more skills to your
profile to start seeing differentiated scores.

**Q: Does applying to more jobs improve my score?**
Not directly. Your match score is based on your profile data, not your
application history. However, applying more helps the AI learn your
preferences over time.

**Q: Can employers see my match score?**
Yes — employers see your match score when they review your application.
It helps them quickly identify the best-matched candidates.

## Applications

**Q: How do I apply for a job?**
Open the job opportunity, tap "Quick Apply", optionally attach a CV or
cover letter, and tap Submit. Your application is sent immediately.

**Q: Can I withdraw an application?**
Yes — go to your Dashboard → My Applications, open the application, and
tap "Withdraw Application". You can only withdraw before the employer
has shortlisted you.

**Q: How long does it take to hear back?**
It depends on the employer. Most employers review applications within
3–7 days of the deadline. You will receive a notification when your
status changes.

**Q: Can I apply to the same job twice?**
No — you can only submit one application per opportunity. If you want to
update your application, withdraw it and reapply.

## CV Generation

**Q: How do I generate a CV?**
Go to Dashboard → Generate CV. Select the sections to include, choose a
template (Professional or Modern), and tap Generate Preview. Download as PDF.

**Q: Can I edit the generated CV?**
The CV is generated from your profile data. To change the content, update
your profile first, then regenerate the CV. You cannot edit the PDF directly
in the app.

**Q: Is the CV free to generate?**
Yes — CV generation is free for all workers.

## Messaging

**Q: How do I message an employer?**
Go to your Dashboard → My Applications, open an application, and tap
"Message Employer". You can also access all conversations from the
Messaging Hub in the bottom navigation.

**Q: Can I send attachments?**
Yes — you can attach documents up to 10MB in size (PDF, Word, images).

**Q: Can employers message me first?**
Yes — if an employer is interested in your profile, they can send you a
message directly.

## Safety & Fraud

**Q: How do I report a suspicious job posting?**
Open the posting and tap the Report button (flag icon). Select the reason
and submit. Our team reviews all reports within 24 hours.

**Q: Will SBOUP ever ask for my password?**
No — SBOUP staff will never ask for your password. If anyone claiming to
be SBOUP asks for your password, it is a scam. Report it immediately.

**Q: Is my personal data safe?**
Yes — SBOUP stores your data securely and does not share it with third
parties without your consent. Your contact details are only visible to
employers when you apply to their posting.
