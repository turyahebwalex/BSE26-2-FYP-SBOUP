# CV Generation on SBOUP

## What is CV generation?

SBOUP can automatically generate a professional CV from your profile data
in seconds. The CV is tailored to highlight the skills and experience most
relevant to the opportunities you are targeting.

You do not need to write a CV from scratch. Everything comes from your
profile — which is another reason to keep your profile complete and
up to date.

## How to generate a CV

1. Go to your **Dashboard**.
2. Tap **Generate CV**.
3. Select which sections to include:
   - Personal information (name, contact, location)
   - Professional summary (from your bio)
   - Skills (choose which skills to highlight)
   - Work experience
   - Education
   - Portfolio links
   - Personality traits
4. Choose a template:
   - **Professional** — clean, formal layout for corporate roles
   - **Modern** — contemporary design for creative and tech roles
5. Tap **Generate Preview** to see the result.
6. Tap **Download** to save as PDF.

## Tailoring your CV for a specific job

For the best results, generate a CV specifically for the job you are
applying to:

1. Open the job opportunity.
2. Tap **Apply** → **Generate Tailored CV**.
3. The system automatically highlights the skills that match the job's
   requirements and reorders sections for relevance.
4. Review and download.

A tailored CV performs significantly better than a generic one because
it shows the employer exactly what they are looking for.

## Tips for a better CV

- **Complete your bio** — it becomes your professional summary. Write it
  as if it is the opening paragraph of your CV.
- **Add portfolio items** — links to your work are included in the CV and
  give employers proof of your skills.
- **List all relevant experience** — even freelance or informal work counts.
  Include the job title, employer, dates, and a brief description.
- **Use correct skill names** — "JavaScript" not "JS", "Carpentry" not
  "Wood Work". Employers scan for specific terms.
- **Keep experience descriptions concise** — 1–2 sentences per role,
  focusing on what you did and what you achieved.

## Downloading and sharing your CV

- CVs are generated as PDF files.
- You can download and share them outside the platform.
- Each time you update your profile, you can regenerate an updated CV.
- Previous CVs are saved in your **My CVs** section.

## What if my CV looks incomplete?

If your generated CV looks thin, it means your profile is missing data.
Go back to your profile and add:
- A bio (becomes the professional summary)
- More skills with proficiency levels
- Work experience entries
- Portfolio items

The more complete your profile, the better your CV.
