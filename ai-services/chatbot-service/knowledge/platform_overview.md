# SBOUP Platform Overview

## What is SBOUP?

SBOUP (Skills-Based Opportunity Unleashing Platform) is a job marketplace
connecting skilled workers with employers across Uganda and East Africa.
Unlike traditional job boards, SBOUP uses AI-powered matching to rank
opportunities by how well they fit each worker's actual skills, experience,
and profile — giving every worker a personalised match score for every job.

## Who is it for?

**Skilled Workers** — anyone with a marketable skill, from software developers
and graphic designers to carpenters, plumbers, tailors, and cooks. If you
have a skill, SBOUP helps you find work that matches it.

**Employers** — businesses, households, and individuals who need to hire.
Employers post opportunities and receive a ranked list of the best-matched
candidates automatically.

## How it works (for workers)

1. Create an account and select the "Skilled Worker" role.
2. Build your profile — add your title, bio, skills, experience, and portfolio.
3. Browse the Discover Jobs feed — every opportunity shows your personal match %.
4. Apply with one tap using Quick Apply.
5. Track your applications from your dashboard.
6. Use the Upskill section to close skill gaps and improve your match scores.
7. Generate a tailored CV for any opportunity.

## How it works (for employers)

1. Create an account and select the "Employer" role.
2. Post an opportunity — add title, description, required skills, location,
   compensation range, and experience level.
3. Receive applications from matched candidates, ranked by match score.
4. Shortlist, message, and hire directly through the platform.

## Key features

- **AI Match Scores** — every worker–job pair gets a score from 0–100 based
  on skill overlap, experience, location, and salary fit.
- **CV Generation** — generate a professional, tailored CV in seconds from
  your profile data.
- **Learning Paths** — identify skill gaps and get recommended free resources
  to close them.
- **Fraud Detection** — AI screening removes suspicious postings automatically.
- **Messaging Hub** — communicate directly with employers or candidates.

## Compensation

All compensation on SBOUP is listed in UGX (Ugandan Shillings).

## Job categories

- **Formal** — permanent or long-term employment
- **Contract** — fixed-term project work
- **Freelance** — flexible, gig-based work
- **Apprenticeship** — learn-while-you-earn placements

## Skill categories

- **Technical** — software development, data, IT
- **Creative** — design, photography, video, content
- **Trade** — carpentry, plumbing, electrical, welding, masonry
- **Business** — accounting, sales, marketing, project management
- **Other** — driving, cooking, tailoring, cleaning, and more
